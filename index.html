About This Campaign

关于本活动

Pittsburgh has a thriving Chinese cultural community most locals walk past without understanding.

匹兹堡拥有蓬勃发展的华人文化社区，但大多数本地人对此知之甚少。

The Culture Spotlight campaign is a multi-platform narrative initiative for the Pittsburgh Chinese Cultural Center (PCCC), designed to bridge the cultural context gap between PCCC's performances and the broader Pittsburgh community.

While PCCC produces vibrant, high-quality cultural events, their current communications function primarily as event recaps for existing community members. This campaign reframes PCCC's content around a curiosity-first, editorial tone that speaks outward to non-heritage audiences — without alienating the existing Chinese American community.

Every component functions as a chapter in one story: a curious Pittsburgher encounters something visually striking, learns what it means, feels welcomed into the community, and eventually advocates for it.

文化聚焦活动是为匹兹堡中华文化中心（PCCC）设计的多平台叙事项目，旨在弥合PCCC演出与更广泛匹兹堡社区之间的文化理解差距。

本活动以好奇心优先的编辑语调重新定位PCCC的内容，向非华裔受众传递文化故事。

"Like a friend explaining their heritage — warm, specific, story-forward."

"就像朋友讲述自己的文化遗产——温暖、具体、故事性强。"

About PCCC

关于PCCC

The community behind the community.

社区背后的社区。

The Pittsburgh Chinese Cultural Center (PCCC) is a nonprofit that promotes Chinese cultural awareness and diversity through involvement with the greater Pittsburgh community. Founded in 2015 following the success of the community-organized Chinese Cultural Festival, PCCC has grown into a platform for anyone — heritage and non-heritage alike — to share talents and exchange culture.

PCCC hosts two major annual events: the Pittsburgh Chinese Cultural Festival and the Greater Pittsburgh Chinese New Year Gala. Their art troupe includes a chorus, Chinese folk-dance team, Qipao team, and drama club, each engaging with Pittsburgh on a variety of stages throughout the year.

Visit pghccc.org to learn more and find upcoming events.

匹兹堡中华文化中心（PCCC）是一个非营利组织，通过参与大匹兹堡社区来促进中国文化意识和多样性。2015年成立以来，PCCC已成为任何人分享才能和交流文化的平台。

访问 pghccc.org 了解更多信息和即将举行的活动。
